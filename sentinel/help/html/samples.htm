Frequency: Daily
Status: Active, no end date.
Reporting Level: 1
Reporting Dates: 06/28/2005 To 06/29/2005
Report Created On: 06/29/2005 4:36:48 AM
Source Database: NCICB QA caDSR

Information in the report is displayed in blocks. Change blocks are indicated by a bright blue color, a whole number under the Group column, and the prefix: "Changes to the ..." The change details appear under the headings "Attribute Name", "Old Value" and "New Value".

  • If no details are available, the text "Details not available" appears.
  • When "Old Value" is "<null>" the attribute was either missing or not set and the "New Value" shows the added value.
  • When "New Value" is "<null>" the attribute has been removed or cleared.
  • When both the old and new values are not "<null>" the attribute value has been changed or replaced.

Associated blocks are indicated by a light blue color, one or more decimal Group numbers, and the prefix "Associated To ...". These blocks indicate information associated to the preceding change block. The first number of the Associated To block corresponds to the related change.

Note all dates are month/day/year (mm/dd/yyyy).
